# 🔒 Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 19, 2024

---

## Privacy First

Lucent Cycle is built on a simple principle: **your data is yours, and it stays on your device.**

We do not collect, store, share, or sell your personal information. Ever.

---

## What We DON'T Do

❌ **No tracking** — No analytics, no pixels, no event logging  
❌ **No servers** — Your data never leaves your browser  
❌ **No accounts** — No login, no email, no identification required  
❌ **No ads** — No advertising or sponsored content  
❌ **No third parties** — No integrations with external services  
❌ **No data collection** — We don't collect anything about you or your cycle  

---

## What Data Is Stored

All data is stored **locally** in your browser using the browser's `localStorage` API:

### Data We Store
- **Period start dates** — The dates you log as the first day of your period
- **Symptom logs** — Daily entries of symptoms (cramps, headache, mood, fatigue)
- **Settings** — Your preferences (cycle length, period length, theme, reminders)

### Data We DO NOT Store
- Your name, email, or identity
- Location or device information
- Browsing history or activity
- Any health data beyond what you explicitly log

---

## How Your Data Works

### Storage
- All data is saved to your device's `localStorage`
- Data persists across browser sessions
- Only you can access your data on that browser/device
- If you sign out or clear your browser cache, the data is deleted

### Browser Sync
- If you use **browser sync** (e.g., Chrome sync, Firefox sync), your Lucent Cycle data may sync to your other devices
- This is controlled by your browser settings, not by us
- We recommend reviewing your browser's privacy settings if you're concerned

### No Server Communication
- Lucent Cycle does **not** communicate with any server
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- All calculations happen locally in your browser

---

## Data Security

Since your data stays on your device:
- **It's as secure as your device** — If your device is compromised, your data could be too
- **No encryption in transit** — Because nothing travels over the network
- **No encryption at rest** — Data is stored as plain text in `localStorage`
- **Browser-level security** — Your browser provides `localStorage` isolation per origin

### Tips for Keeping Your Data Safe
1. **Use a strong device password** — This protects all your local data
2. **Keep your browser updated** — Security patches protect `localStorage`
3. **Back up your data manually** — We're working on export functionality
4. **Don't share your device** — Or use private browsing if you do
5. **Review browser permissions** — Ensure only trusted browser extensions can access `localStorage`

## Your Rights

### Accessing Your Data
You own your data. You can:
- View all your stored data in your browser's Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage
- Manually export or copy your data anytime
- Understand exactly what we store (see "What Data Is Stored" above)

### Deleting Your Data
You have full control:
- **Within the app** → Use Settings → "Clear all data" to wipe everything at once
- **Browser level** → Clear your browser cache/history to delete all Lucent Cycle data
- **Selective** → Your browser's Developer Tools let you delete specific `localStorage` entries
